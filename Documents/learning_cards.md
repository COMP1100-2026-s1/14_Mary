Learning Card 1 

 

We believe that UQ students have issues during the course selection process which could be amended by us using technology. 

 

We observed that 85% of the students that were interviewed had trouble with the course selection process as a direct result of how scattered the relevant information was in the current UQ resources. 

 

We have learned that to make the course selection process easier all of the information that is relevant to picking courses should be centralized in one space to eliminate potential confusion by students.
