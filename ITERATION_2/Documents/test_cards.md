## Hypothesis 3 (Iteration 2)

**HYPOTHESIS**: We believe that a major and surprisingly common pain point UQ students regarding the UQ enrolment system is the lack of context and information when selecting courses, or at least a lack of convenience and clarity when looking up the appropriate sources. 

**TEST**: To validate our hypothesis, we will ask UQ students specifically and more in-depth questions about
* What their current information sources for selecting courses are, and if they are provided, endorsed or recommended by UQ, or third-party external resources
* If their current UQ-affiliated sources are satisfactory or sufficient in the information/context/instruction they provide
* If their current non-UQ-affiliated sources (if any) are satisfactory or sufficient in the information/context/instruction they provide
* How their current personal enrollment process- or mind- flow goes, and what sources/sites they reference for information
* What their first enrollment process was like, and how they found or experimented with the proper steps & sources to complete the enrollment process and select their courses correctly
* If they have any particular bad experiences, mistakes or better information sources, that they would like to see changes to UQ's current system to be based on

**METRIC**: We will assess the stated quality of both UQ and non-UQ sources of information students use, and see what exactly it is about UQ's system or the external sources that make them dissatisfied with the former or more inclined towards the latter

**CRITERIA**: Our hypothesis is valid if the student's responses majorly affirm that there are indeed weaknesses about the current UQ enrollment or information system, and/or if there are external sources that address common issues students want to avoid with the current official system.

4.Which information regarding courses was hard to locate or understand, for instance, workload or course difficulty level?

5.What sources or individuals did you depend on to aid you in making your choices regarding courses?

6.If the enrolment system assisted you in completing the task, what form of guidance would have helped you?
